Learning Assessment Index

Learning Assessment Structure

Our assessment policy is designed to provide regular feedback on student progress. It consists of:

  • - Continuous Assessment: 40% of the total score (Classwork, Projects, Tests)
  • - End of Term Examination: 60% of the total score

Reports are issued termly to provide a comprehensive overview of academic performance, behavioral traits, and participation in extracurricular activities. This ensures parents are well-informed partners in their child's educational journey.
